Our Services

Digital Transformation Services

  • Application Development and Integrations

    Specializing in implementing S4HANA on Public/Private Cloud, we build robust applications and integrate them seamlessly with your existing systems for enhanced efficiency.

  • Mobile Development

    Empower your workforce and customers with easy application access through intuitive Mobile Apps, ensuring productivity and engagement on the go.

  • Business Technology Platform

    Harness the power of "Platform as a Service" (PaaS) with our Business Technology Platform solutions, providing a flexible and scalable foundation for your digital initiatives.

  • SAP Analytics Cloud

    Gain comprehensive insights through powerful analytical reports, interactive dashboards, and advanced planning capabilities with our SAP Analytics Cloud expertise.

Application Maintenance Services

ACS features a skilled team committed to providing superior SAP AMS support for optimal SAP system performance and stability.

  • Onsite Model

    Benefit from dedicated resources physically present at your client site, providing direct and immediate support tailored to your specific needs.

  • Offsite Model

    Utilize dedicated resources working from an offsite location, offering cost-effective and efficient support while maintaining close communication and collaboration.

  • Offsite Shared Model

    Experience flexible and scalable support based on key business processes, with shared offsite resources optimizing cost and expertise across various clients.

  • Hybrid Support Model

    Combine the best of both worlds with an onsite lead managing a seamless offsite delivery team, ensuring effective communication and efficient execution.
