Our Industry Focus

Palm Oil Industry

Driving sustainable growth and operational excellence in palm oil with SAP.

The palm oil industry navigates complex challenges, from efficiently managing plantations to meeting strict sustainability standards like RSPO and ISPO. SAP’s integrated solutions enable palm oil companies to optimize every facet of their operations — spanning estate, mill, refinery, and global markets.

Key Capabilities:

  • Plantation & Harvest Management: SAP helps monitor activities such as planting, fertilization, and harvesting, while tracking fresh fruit bunch (FFB) yields by estate and block, delivering accurate cost and performance insights to boost productivity.
  • Mill & Asset Optimization: Scheduling and overseeing mill and equipment maintenance minimizes downtime. Tools to monitor oil extraction rates (OER) and free fatty acids (FFA) help maintain quality.
  • Logistics & Traceability: Coordinates transportation from plantation to mill, reducing spoilage and ensuring full traceability of palm oil batches, vital for RSPO audits and compliance.
  • Financial & Commodity Risk Management: Automated accounting and granular cost tracking support profitability, while tools for managing contracts and prices tied to global CPO indices help mitigate market risks.
  • Sustainability & ESG: Built-in tools monitor emissions, waste, and KPIs, streamlining RSPO, ISPO, and GRI reporting.

Semiconductors & HPC

Implementing SAP S/4HANA for the semiconductor and high-performance computing sector.

Ajha consultancy services PVT Ltd worked with SAP delivery partner to implement the S4hana for Penguin Solutions, offering advanced computing solutions for AI, HPC, and data analytics, classified under Semiconductors & High-Performance Computing. The solution was designed for FICO and SD processes with a focus on RAR – revenue recognition.

SAP’s Solutions for the Semiconductor & HPC Industry:

  • SAP Integrated Business Planning (IBP) for demand balancing.
  • SAP Digital Manufacturing (SAP DM) integrates with MES for fabs and OSATs providing real-time dashboards.
  • SAP Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) and Electronic Product Design (EPD) for secure, global collaboration.
  • S4HANA for Semiconductor Financials with wafer costing & lot-based accounting.
  • SAP Asset Performance Management (APM) for predictive maintenance of high-value equipment.
  • SAP Sustainability Control Tower to monitor carbon emissions.
