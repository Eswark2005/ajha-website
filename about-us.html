About Us

About Us

Ajha Consulting Service Pvt Ltd is a dynamic startup founded in 2022 by a team of seasoned SAP consultants, united by a passion to deliver cutting-edge technology services for ERP implementations and maintenance. Headquartered in Bangalore, India, with a registered presence in Texas, USA, we proudly serve clients across the globe.

Our Story

Ajha Consulting was established by Ram Annadanam, a visionary leader with over 20 years of SAP consulting experience, an MBA, and a deep understanding of diverse industry landscapes. This journey fuelled his mission: harnessing innovative technologies — AI, automation, ERP cloud, SAP BTP, and MuleSoft integrations — to transform how businesses evolve.

What We Do

We provide cost-effective ERP solutions, from greenfield implementations to optimising existing systems. Our seasoned consultants help SMEs achieve operational excellence by using the latest tools and methods that reduce long-term maintenance costs.

Our Promise

To keep our clients' technology solutions at their best — modern, efficient, and ready for tomorrow — with minimal upkeep costs.

Our Growth & Partnerships

We’ve delivered solutions for Navbharath Limited and Shriram Properties, and partner with leading SAP providers to offer robust cloud ERP public solutions, expanding our reach and impact.

Our Belief

"To succeed in your mission, you must have single-minded devotion to your goal." — A principle our founder Ram Annadanam and the team live by every day, driving us to deliver unmatched value to our clients.
