About Us

About Us

Ajha Consulting Service Pvt Ltd is a dynamic startup founded in 2023 by a team of seasoned SAP consultants, united by a passion to deliver cutting-edge technology services for ERP implementations and maintenance. Headquartered in Bangalore, India, with a registered presence in Texas, USA, we proudly serve clients across the globe.

Our Story

Ajha Consulting was established by Ram Annadanam, a visionary leader with over 20 years of SAP consulting experience, an MBA, and a deep understanding of diverse industry landscapes. Throughout his extensive career, Ram has designed tailored industry solutions and drawn inspiration from global business leaders. This journey fuelled his mission: to harness the power of innovative technologies—from AI and automation to intelligent ERP cloud solutions, SAP Business Technology Platform, and MuleSoft integrations—to transform the way businesses evolve.

What We Do

At Ajha Consulting, we specialize in providing cost-effective ERP solutions, whether it's a greenfield implementation or streamlining your existing systems for optimal performance. Our seasoned consultants focus on helping small and medium enterprises achieve operational excellence by leveraging the latest tools and methodologies that reduce long-term maintenance costs.

Our Promise

Our mission is simple yet powerful:
To keep our clients' technology solutions at their best—modern, efficient, and ready for tomorrow—while ensuring minimal upkeep costs.

Our Growth & Partnerships

Since our inception, we've successfully delivered enhancement and implementation solutions for clients such as Navbharath Limited and Shriram Properties. We also collaborate closely with leading SAP partners to bring robust cloud ERP public solutions to our clients, expanding our impact and reach.

Our Belief

"To succeed in your mission, you must have single-minded devotion to your goal." — A principle our founder Ram Annadanam and the team live by every day, driving us with focus and dedication to navigate the challenges of entrepreneurship and deliver unparalleled value to our clients.
